Lal Mahal

Discription

Lal Mahal,the "red palace" is a historical site in pune,built in 1630 by Shahaji Bhosale for his wife Jijabai and son,Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It served as their residence until1645 and is famous for an encounter where shivaji cut off Shaista Khan's fingers while he was escaping.The currect structure is a reconstruction by the Pune Municipal Corporation and features paitings and a statue of Jijabai, along with a sculpture of Shiwaji plowing with a golden plough.
